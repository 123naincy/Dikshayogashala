Yogic Food and Accommodation at Dikhshayogashala School

At Dikhshayogashala School, we believe that a healthy body is essential for a flourishing spirit. Just as our physical well-being depends on a safe and hygienic environment, our surroundings play a crucial role in our overall health. Our school is nestled in a serene location, surrounded by lush green mountains and tranquil fields, providing the perfect backdrop for your yoga journey.

Accommodation

At Dikhsayogashala, we prioritize your comfort and well-being by offering serene accommodations that complement your yoga teacher training journey. Choose from twin-shared or private rooms, each thoughtfully designed with modern amenities to ensure a restful and peaceful stay. Our rooms feature cozy beds, clean, well-maintained bathrooms, and a calming atmosphere that allows you to unwind and recharge after your daily training sessions. The tranquil environment encourages you to disconnect from distractions and fully immerse yourself in the yogic lifestyle. To further enhance your experience, we also offer limited air-conditioned rooms at an additional cost, so be sure to inquire early to secure your preferred option. With everything you need for a hassle-free stay, Dikhsayogashala provides the perfect space to relax, rejuvenate, and focus entirely on your growth as a yoga practitioner.

Yogic Food

The food we consume profoundly influences our mental and physical well-being. For yogis, a balanced and nutritious diet is vital. At Dikhshayogashala School, we emphasize a yogic diet that draws on the principles of natural healing, rejuvenation, and energy, inspired by the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda. This diet is crafted to nourish not only the body but also the mind and spirit, enhancing the flexibility and stamina necessary for your practice.
