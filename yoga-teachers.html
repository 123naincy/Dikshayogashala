Rahul Nautiyal

Yoga Teacher

A dedicated Yoga Instructor with over four years of experience, Rahul is deeply passionate about sharing the transformative power of yoga. Raised in Rishikesh, the Yoga Capital of the World, and a Post Graduate in Yoga from Uttarakhand Open University, Rahul is well-versed in various styles of yoga, including Ashtanga, Vinyasa, and Multi-Style Yoga. He is committed to providing comprehensive guidance and support to his students, helping them find inner peace and optimal health. With a deep understanding of yoga asanas and breathing techniques, Rahul is adept at working with people of all ages and experience levels.

Khushbu Bhardwaj

Yoga Teacher

Khushbu Bhardwaj is a passionate yoga practitioner and teacher with over eight years of experience. She is skilled in various yoga practices, including pranayama, meditation, and chanting. Her academic journey includes a Master's degree in Yogic Science from Uttarakhand Sanskrit University and a certification from Yoga Alliance, USA. Khushbu has conducted workshops and retreats, performed in international music and yoga projects, and has even worked as a counselor and life coach. Currently, she focuses on sharing her knowledge and expertise with students, guiding them towards a healthier and more balanced lifestyle through yoga and meditation.

Krishna Ji

Yoga Teacher

Hailing from the spiritual hub of Rishikesh, India, Krishna Ji is a dedicated yoga practitioner and teacher. With a strong foundation in both Hatha and Ashtanga Yoga, she has been guiding students on their yoga journey for over seven years. Krishna Ji's passion for yoga is evident in her classes, which cater to both beginners and advanced practitioners. She skillfully blends traditional yoga techniques with modern insights, creating a dynamic and transformative yoga experience. Her commitment to sharing the benefits of yoga is evident in her dedication to teaching and her focus on creating a strong, focused, and truthful yoga practice. With her deep understanding of yoga philosophy and practice, Krishna Ji empowers her students to connect with their inner selves and achieve physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Surendar Kumar

Yoga Teacher

Rishabh Prajapati

Yoga Teacher

Rishabh is a Writer, Thinker, Seeker, Learner, Astrologer, and Mental health enthusiast. A former copywriter and brand consultant, he has been called back to yoga—a path he began as a child. Rishabh's yoga journey started at the age of 12 at Parmarth Niketan, where he learned the foundational practices of yoga along with his cousins. He later deepened his practice at Sivananda Ashram. Although he took a brief hiatus from yoga during his academic years, he rekindled his passion by earning a diploma in yoga from Haridwar Sanskrit University. While pursuing a career in writing and advertising, Rishabh continued his education, completing a Bachelor’s and Master’s in Yoga from Uttarakhand Open University. Recently, Rishabh participated in a Hatha Yoga Teacher Training Course to update his knowledge and reconnect with his yogic path. Through asana, anatomy and philosophy in yoga, he seeks not only to continue his own journey of self-discovery but also to help others recognize the voice of their inner self and align with their true essence.

Gangesha Chaitanya

Yoga Teacher

Discover the transformative teachings of Yogi Gangesha Chaitanya, a master of Yoga Philosophy, Meditation, Pranayama, and Spiritual Guidance. From a young age, he was drawn to spirituality and flourished under the guidance of the esteemed Swami Rama. His spiritual path took him to renowned institutions like the Himalayan tradition, Sri Aurobindo Ashram, Brahma Kumaris, and the Maha Yana Buddhist tradition, where he embraced a rich blend of ancient and modern wisdom. Specializing in meditation, Kundalini yoga, and pranayama, Yogi Gangesha inspires his students to awaken their dormant energy and elevate their consciousness. His unique teaching style combines tradition with innovation, creating a serene and compassionate space for self-discovery. Join Yogi Gangesha Chaitanya on a journey of inner peace and enlightenment, and unlock the true potential of your mind and spirit.
