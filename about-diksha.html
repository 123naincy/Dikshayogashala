About Diksha Yoga Shala

Accredited by Yoga Alliance, USA, Diksha Yogshala is the best and most reputed yoga school in the world. Instituted in the lap of nature, our yoga school is known for its tranquility and holistic well-being converge. Yoga learning here is a holistic approach that helps in the spiritual, mental, and physical transformation of the mind, body, and soul. Moreover, in our school of yoga, we focus to connect ourselves with the supreme power of the world.

A team of trained, certified, and experienced yoga Gurus of our yoga school helps you in understanding and practicing the art of yoga that rejuvenates the three crucial segments of the human body, i,e. body, mind, and soul. They also guide you throughout your journey of inner harmony and self-discovery. In our traditional yoga school, we ensure a nurturing and safe yoga learning atmosphere for our students.

Enveloped by lush green surroundings, the free-flowing River Ganga, majestic mountain ranges, and a pollution-free environment push you to immerse yourself in the majestic beauty of yoga. From the ancient traditions of Hatha and Ashtanga to the meditation practices of Kundalini and Yin, we offer detailed and various yoga styles. During your YTTC in Diksha Yogshala, we help you in exploring various aspects of yoga such as philosophy, Ayurveda, and Meditation.

It is the perfect setting for self-transformation. Transmitting the spirit of Rishikesh and its Yogic tradition within, World Peace Yoga School is in the heart of Rishikesh. We offer our students the best facilities, environment, and ambiance to receive only the very best Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh. This has been largely due to the position World Peace Yoga School has developed around the world as one of the best Yoga Teacher Training Schools in Rishikesh. Internationally and on home soil all those keen to further develop their Yoga Teaching Teacher Training and spiritual growth come to World Peace Yoga School. All our Teacher Training Courses (TTC) are certified and authorized by Yoga Alliance and its subsidiaries, including Yoga Alliance in the USA.

If you are taking a trip to Rishikesh as a tourist and not yet ready to commence your yoga journey why not come visit us and find out more. World Peace Yoga School also invites people, of all backgrounds and levels to visit any of our Yoga Schools in Rishikesh, get a sense of our facilities, environment, and atmosphere and see why we're considered one of the best Yoga Teacher Training Schools in Rishikesh. Our well-educated and trained crew of yoga teachers is always ready to talk to you through our range of Yoga Teacher Training Courses (TTC), which include Hatha Yoga Teacher Training, Ashtanga Yoga Teacher Training, and Vinyasa Yoga. We are happy to take any questions or concerns that you may have

Vision and Mission

The Diksha Yoga Shala is a part of the Holistic Health Care foundation (HHF). HHF is an NGO working with a mission and vision. As an organisation we have a dream to see the humanity free from grief and misery where peace prosperity and happiness prevail forever. We understand that it is herculean job to achieve that state of total liberation for the humanity at large. Here, The Diksha Yoga Shala strongly believes that million miles long journey starts with a single step. We are quite optimistic that one day we will be able to create a wave of people who think globally and act locally to serve the humanity better.

Our Mission

“To be a beacon of holistic wellness, empowering women with PCOS and fertility challenges to embrace the transformative power of yoga and a healthy diet for a balanced, healthier life.”

Our Vision

Why Choose Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh at Diksha Yogashala?

"Begin Your Transformative Yoga Journey at Diksha Yogashala in Rishikesh"

At Diksha Yogashala, we offer an authentic and immersive Yoga Teacher Training experience in the spiritual heart of Rishikesh. Surrounded by the majestic Himalayas and the sacred Ganges, our comprehensive program is designed to nurture your personal growth, deepen your practice, and empower you to become a confident yoga teacher.

Authentic Yoga Experience: Immerse yourself in the birthplace of yoga, where ancient traditions come to life.

Serene and Spiritual Setting: Train in the peaceful foothills of the Himalayas, next to the sacred Ganges River.

Sattvic Food and Accommodation: Enjoy wholesome, sattvic meals and comfortable accommodation, designed to enhance your physical and mental well-being.

Comprehensive Curriculum: Master asanas, pranayama, meditation, and philosophy, designed for all levels.

Yoga Alliance USA Certification: Receive globally recognized certification, allowing you to teach yoga anywhere in the world.

Personal and Spiritual Growth: Transform your life through a holistic approach to yoga, focusing on healing and self-love.

Experienced and Supportive Instructors: Learn from seasoned teachers committed to your personal and professional development.

Rishikesh and Village Exploration: Experience the charm of nearby villages, immerse yourself in local culture, and explore the spiritual essence of Rishikesh.

Global Yoga Community: Connect with like-minded individuals from around the world, fostering a supportive environment.

Blend of Tradition and Modernity: Experience a unique combination of ancient yogic wisdom and contemporary teaching techniques.

At Diksha Yogashala, yoga is more than just a practice—it's a transformative journey. Rishikesh, the spiritual heart of the world, offers an environment that nurtures both personal and professional growth. Surrounded by nature's beauty, with the sound of temple bells and the sacred Ganges flowing nearby, you'll feel the spiritual energy that infuses every aspect of your training.

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to take you from beginner to advanced, ensuring that you gain both theoretical knowledge and practical experience. Whether you're looking to deepen your personal practice or step into teaching, our experienced instructors provide guidance every step of the way.

Join Diksha Yogashala and be part of a global community of seekers and practitioners, all united in the pursuit of inner peace and self-discovery. This is not just a certification—it's a life-changing journey.
